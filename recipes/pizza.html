Pizza

Description

A pizza! who doesnt love it. in this simple recipe you will learn how to make your very own pizza (dough)

Ingredients

  • 1 package of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of warm water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and put the oven on 230C.
  2. place water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix it and let it stand until its creamy, about 10 min.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
  4. Let it all rest for 5 minutes.
  5. put the dough mixture on a surface with some flour on it so it doesnt stick and pat or roll it out into a pizza shape.
  6. spread your pizza with toppings of your choosing.
  7. put the pizza in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
